About Us


Welcome to JK Foods, where passion meets flavor!


At JK Foods, we believe that dining is more than just food—it's an experience. Established in 2002, our restaurant has been serving the velachery,chennai community with a commitment to quality, hospitality, and culinary innovation. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring people together over great food in a warm, welcoming environment.

Food Menu

Idly

Idly is not just a meal; it’s a celebration of health and tradition.Steamed to perfection, these round, spongy rice cakes are: Packed with carbohydrates, proteins, and vitamins.

Price: ₹ 150

Dosa

dosa. Crisp, golden, and delightfully thin, dosa is a savory crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram). It’s a perfect combination of crunch on the outside and a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture inside, offering a culinary experience like no other.

Price: ₹ 170

Pongal

A creamy, savory dish made with rice and moong dal, tempered with cumin, peppercorns, and ghee. It offers a comforting, rich flavor that is both hearty and soothing.

Price: ₹ 190

Uttapam

Soft, thick pancake-like dosa topped with onions, tomatoes, and green chilies. The toppings caramelize during cooking, adding a sweet and savory flavor to the spongy uttapam. "Loaded with love, topped with tradition!"

Price: ₹ 200

Appam

Soft, fluffy center with crispy edges. Its slight sweetness pairs beautifully with coconut-based curries or vegetable stew. "Crispy edges, soft centers—tradition on a plate!"

Price: ₹ 220

Bisi Bele Bath

A hearty and wholesome rice-lentil dish with vegetables, flavored with tamarind and spices. Every spoonful is warm, comforting, and rich in flavor. "Hot, hearty, and bursting with flavor!"

Price: ₹ 230

Kerala Parotta

Flaky, layered flatbread that’s crispy outside and soft inside. Perfect when paired with spicy curries or stews.

Price: ₹ 250

Biriyani

Biryani is a fragrant, flavorful rice dish that is a feast for both the senses and the soul. Each mouthful delivers an explosion of spices, herbs, and marinated meat (chicken, mutton, or fish), or vegetables. "Layered with flavor, crafted with love!"

Price: ₹ 250

Curd Rice

A simple, refreshing dish made from rice mixed with yogurt and tempered with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. Cooling and soothing. "Cool, creamy, and utterly refreshing!"

Price: ₹ 170

Rava Kesari

A sweet dish made with semolina, ghee, sugar, and flavored with saffron. It’s smooth, rich, and melts in your mouth with every bite. "Golden sweetness in every spoonful!"

Price: ₹ 160
